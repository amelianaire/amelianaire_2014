Virtuoso Mobile Site

By building a consumer-focused mobile site, we increased overall conversions by 25%.

Overview

The company had a B2B mobile site despite the fact that the majority of mobile traffic was consumers. Our scrappy team of 4 rebuilt the site with a consumer focus as a side project from our sprint work. We introduced new styles that were later integrated to desktop. I focused on the cruise and "Find an Advisor" flows.

Approach

  • Focus on our most popular consumer content.
  • Weave the value of working with a travel advisor through each landing page, and recommend advisors in a relevant way as you browse hotel, cruise and tour products.
  • Completely redesign look and feel to elevate the brand and match our luxury offerings.
  • Test user flows throughout the 9-month process and integrate feedback.

Results

  • 20% of all Virtuoso.com consumer conversions now come from mobile devices.
  • Estimated $10,000 additional network revenue in just the first 3 weeks.
