Virtuoso Mobile Site

By building a consumer-focused mobile site, we increased overall conversions by 25%.

Overview

Since consumers were the majority of our mobile traffic, we scrapped our B2B mobile site (which had been built years before by a lone developer) and set out to build an effective experience for consumers. A side project from our sprint work, we kept the focus very small to ensure we could deliver the most important consumer content with our limited resources (three UX designers and one developer). As a bonus, we used the opportunity to introduce new styles which would later be integrated to desktop. While it was a highly collaborative effort, I focused on the cruise and "Find an Advisor" flows.

Approach

  • Focus on our most popular consumer content.
  • Weave the value of working with a travel advisor through each landing page, and recommend advisors in a relevant way as you browse hotel, cruise and tour products.
  • Completely redesign look and feel to elevate the brand and match our luxury offerings.
  • Test user flows throughout the 9-month process and integrate feedback.

Results

  • 20% of all Virtuoso.com consumer conversions now come from mobile devices.
  • Estimated $10,000 additional network revenue in just the first 3 weeks.
