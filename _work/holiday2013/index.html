2013 Engineering School Holiday Card

The simplest holiday tasks become a lot more exciting when an engineer is involved.

Project Details

The 2013 holiday card illustrated typical winter traditions reimagined through the eyes of engineers. While anyone who knows an engineer will get the joke, the audience for this card was key donors, alumni and partners (mostly engineers).

For the larger alumni audience, we sent an HTML email with a link to the card. Since more and more people see emails first on their phone, we decided to make the site responsive.
