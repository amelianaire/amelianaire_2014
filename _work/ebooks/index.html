E-book Launch Poster Series

Inspired by vintage travel posters, this e-book announcement campaign celebrated the ease of taking books anywhere.

Summary

Books have always taken us to exotic places, but e-books allow us to take all of our books along with us. These playful illustrations imagine well-known books (which were available in our collection) in environments relevant to their stories.
