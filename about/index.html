--- title: Amelia Johnson - About ---

I love design because I enjoy solving problems.

I’m a detail-oriented designer with ten years of experience crafting effective and accessible user-centered work. Currently, I'm a senior UX designer at Expedia.

I love defining the root problem, outlining clear goals, researching, iterating, gathering feedback and collaborating across disciplines. Typically, I've got a self-initiated project or two outside my product work to address pain points within my team.

Outside of work, I spend time traveling with my family near (16 national parks and counting) and far (French Polynesia and Iceland's Ring Road are recent favorites), reading non-fiction, and studying opera.
