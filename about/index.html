Summary

I’m a detail-oriented designer with six years of experience crafting online experiences. Currently I'm a UX designer at Virtuoso, a travel company.

My favorite projects are those where I find ways to translate complex business rules into understandable UI. I believe the web should be accessible to all.

Aside from design, I have experience in writing for web, prototyping in HTML and CSS, and a knack for articulating points best practices of web writing, experience with writing effective web copy, following usability and accessibility best practices I've been on small, “we-do-it-all” teams where I picked up new skills from collaborating with my colleagues how to write web copy, effective marketing strategy, and even creating multimedia content.
